# Kingston Beta Fidelity Rules

These rules exist to prevent future drift away from the supplied reference image. Treat the reference as the source of truth.

## Elements That Must Never Change

Never remove or fundamentally alter:
- Ivory-dominant background.
- Kingston Beta logo in the top header.
- Lime active navigation tab descending from the top edge.
- Dot separators between navigation items.
- Social square buttons at top right.
- Left hamburger/utility rail.
- Vertical rail copy: `START. CONNECT. GROW.`
- Giant condensed uppercase hero headline.
- Black first headline lines and lime final headline lines.
- Small support copy placed beside the headline mass.
- Primary filled lime CTA and secondary lime-outline CTA.
- Abstract `K` collage on the right.
- Dotted lime matrix accents.
- Bottom soft stats band.

## Compositional Anchors

Anchor 1: Header
- Full-width.
- Logo sits left after hamburger rail.
- Active nav tab is vertically prominent.
- Social buttons sit on the far right.

Anchor 2: Headline
- Left-center dominant.
- Four stacked lines.
- Must be the largest typographic object.

Anchor 3: Abstract `K`
- Right-side visual identity object.
- Must be larger than any normal section image.
- Must combine image masks and lime geometry.

Anchor 4: Stats Band
- Low in the viewport.
- Soft translucent surface.
- Four evenly spaced impact groups.

## Hierarchy Anchors

Visual priority order:
1. Hero headline.
2. Abstract `K` collage.
3. Logo/header navigation.
4. CTA buttons.
5. Support copy.
6. Stats band.
7. Decorative dots/matrices.

Do not make CTAs, cards, forms, or secondary sections louder than the H1 and `K` collage.

## Image Placement Rules

Images must:
- Live inside the abstract `K` structure or editorial masked shapes.
- Include human community moments and Jamaican place context.
- Preserve tight editorial cropping.
- Avoid normal rectangular grid placement in the hero.

Hero image structure:
- One tall left stroke photo mask.
- One central angled city/context mask.
- One right portrait/community mask.
- Lime shapes completing the `K`.
- White diagonal cuts separating masks.

Do not:
- Use a single full-bleed background photo.
- Use standard image cards.
- Use a generic collage unrelated to the `K`.
- Use generated assets from rejected prior directions.

## Spacing Preservation Rules

Maintain:
- Large ivory negative space around the headline.
- Clear separation between headline and right collage.
- Dedicated left rail width.
- Header breathing room.
- Stats band separated from CTAs.
- Small support copy block with short line length.

Do not:
- Center all content.
- Compress the headline into a narrow mobile-style block on desktop.
- Let the `K` collage collide with headline text.
- Fill every empty area with content.

## Typography Preservation Rules

Hero headline:
- Must be uppercase.
- Must use condensed heavy sans-serif.
- Must have tight line-height.
- Must preserve black-to-lime line contrast.

Navigation:
- Small, clean, evenly spaced.
- Dot separators required.
- Active state is a block, not underline-only.

Buttons:
- Letter-spaced uppercase feel.
- Long horizontal proportions.
- Arrow detail preserved.

Stats:
- Lime numbers.
- Compact labels.
- Muted descriptive text.

Do not:
- Use rounded playful fonts.
- Use generic Inter-only hero type if it loses the condensed poster feel.
- Convert H1 to sentence-case marketing copy.
- Use oversized body copy that competes with H1.

## Responsive Preservation Rules

Desktop must preserve the full poster composition.

Tablet may:
- Reduce headline size.
- Compress nav spacing.
- Scale the `K` collage.
- Wrap stats into two columns.

Mobile may:
- Collapse nav.
- Stack support copy below H1.
- Move `K` collage below the headline.
- Stack stats vertically.

Mobile must not:
- Become a generic centered SaaS hero.
- Remove the condensed uppercase headline.
- Replace the `K` collage with a plain rectangle.
- Remove brand dots entirely.
- Turn the palette into a dark theme.

## Anti-Redesign Rules

Do not reinterpret the reference as:
- A SaaS site.
- A startup landing page.
- A dashboard.
- A generic meetup platform.
- A conference website.
- A tech-product homepage.

Do not add:
- Pricing.
- Feature grid.
- Dashboard screenshots.
- App mockups.
- Generic testimonials.
- Dark hero gradients.
- Purple/blue tech graphics.
- Glass cards.
- Floating UI widgets.

## Color Fidelity Rules

Required:
- Ivory background remains dominant.
- Lime remains the energetic brand signal.
- Black remains the headline authority.
- Red appears only as a dot-level accent.
- Forest green supports icons and details.

Forbidden:
- Neon lime.
- Blue/purple gradients.
- Black/dark-mode backgrounds.
- Beige/brown-heavy palette.
- Overuse of red.

## Motion Fidelity Rules

Motion may enhance:
- Header entry.
- Active nav tab drop.
- Headline mask reveal.
- `K` image mask reveal.
- Dot matrix fade.
- Stats band entrance.
- Subtle scroll parallax.

Motion must not:
- Bounce.
- Scale aggressively.
- Animate every card.
- Feel playful or dashboard-like.
- Hide content for reduced-motion users.

## Implementation Drift Checklist

Before accepting any future implementation, verify:
- Does the first viewport still look like the supplied reference?
- Is the H1 still the dominant object?
- Is the right side still an abstract `K` collage?
- Is the background still mostly ivory?
- Is lime used with the same discipline?
- Are dots and social squares still present?
- Is the active nav a vertical block?
- Are stats soft, horizontal, and editorial on desktop?
- Does the page avoid SaaS/dashboard/startup tropes?

If any answer is no, stop and correct the implementation before continuing.
